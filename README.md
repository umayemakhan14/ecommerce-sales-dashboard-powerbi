# 🌍 Global E-Commerce Sales Dashboard  

### Transforming Raw Transactions into Business Decisions  

An interactive **3-page Power BI dashboard** designed to uncover hidden patterns in global e-commerce operations by analyzing sales trends, product profitability, regional performance, and payment behavior.

This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through **data storytelling, interactive visuals, and KPI tracking**.

---

## ✨ Project Story

A growing e-commerce company was generating thousands of transactions but struggled to answer critical business questions:

- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which regions drive maximum sales?
- Are payment failures affecting revenue?
- Which products need improvement?
- How can leadership track everything in one place?

To solve this, I built a centralized Power BI dashboard that helps decision-makers monitor business performance in real time.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Architecture

### 1️⃣ Sales Overview Dashboard
Provides a high-level business snapshot.

**Includes:**
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Orders  
- Average Order Value  
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Sales by Category  
- Profit by Category  
- Top 10 Products  

---

### 2️⃣ Product Performance Dashboard
Deep dive into product-level analytics.

**Includes:**
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Quantity  
- Top Products by Profit  
- Quantity Sold by Product  
- Sales by Subcategory  
- Bottom Performing Products  

---

### 3️⃣ Regional & Payment Dashboard
Tracks global expansion and transaction efficiency.

**Includes:**
- Total Sales  
- Failed Payments  
- Completed Payments  
- Sales by Country Map  
- Sales by Continent  
- Payment Method Distribution  
- Payment Status Analysis
